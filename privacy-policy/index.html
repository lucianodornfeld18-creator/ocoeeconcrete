Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

Ocoee Concrete & Pavers ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website ocoeeconcrete.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us for services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information you voluntarily provide: name, phone number, email address, and project details submitted through our contact form or WhatsApp

How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

WhatsApp Communications

Your Rights
