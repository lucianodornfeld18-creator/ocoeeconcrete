Do you serve Winter Garden and Windermere? +

Yes! We serve all of West Orange County including Winter Garden, Windermere, Oakland, Clermont and surrounding areas. We also cover Kissimmee, Davenport and Four Corners.

How long does paver installation take? +

Most residential paver projects are completed in 2–5 days. Larger driveways or complex designs may take up to 7 days. We always give you a timeline before starting.

Are pavers better than concrete for Florida's climate? +

Pavers handle Florida's heat, rain and occasional freeze-thaw much better than poured slabs. They flex naturally, drain better, and individual pieces can be replaced without breaking up an entire surface.
