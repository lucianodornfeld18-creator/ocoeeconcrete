FAQ

Paver Patio FAQ

How much does a paver patio cost in Central Florida?

A standard 300 sq ft patio in Central Florida runs $5,000–$9,500 in concrete pavers or $6,500–$12,000 in travertine. Larger patios (400–600 sq ft) range $9,500–$14,000. We provide free on-site estimates.

Can pavers be installed over existing concrete?

Yes — in many cases we install pavers over an existing concrete patio if the slab is structurally sound. This saves $1,000–$3,000 in demolition costs. We evaluate during the free estimate.

How long does a paver patio take to install?

Most residential patios are completed in 2–4 days. Larger or more complex projects with lighting or fire pit integration may take 5–7 days. We give you a firm timeline before starting.

Do paver patios hold up in Florida's rain?

Yes — the joint spacing between pavers allows water to drain through rather than pool on the surface. This is a significant advantage over concrete patios in Florida's heavy summer rain season. We also grade the base with a slight slope away from the house for additional drainage.

What's the best way to add privacy to a paver patio?

We can include raised paver retaining walls (12–24" high) around the perimeter of your patio as a natural privacy solution. These also create a defined outdoor room feel. Pergola bases on the paver surface allow you to add shade structures later.
