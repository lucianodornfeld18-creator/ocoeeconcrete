Paver FAQ — Winter Garden, FL

How much does paver installation cost in Winter Garden?

Paver driveways in Winter Garden typically run $9,000–$20,000 and patios $3,500–$11,000. Travertine pool decks and larger projects can go higher. We provide free on-site estimates.

Do you serve Hamlin, Watermark and Stoneybrook West?

Yes — we've completed dozens of projects in those communities. We know the HOA standards and local drainage characteristics well.

Can you add LED lighting to my paver project?

Yes! We install LED step lights, pathway lights and deck lighting as an add-on. It's a popular upgrade in Watermark and Hamlin homes.

Pavers vs travertine — which is better for Winter Garden?

Both are excellent. Concrete pavers are durable and cost-effective with many color options. Travertine is a natural stone that stays cooler underfoot — ideal for pool decks near Florida's lakes.
