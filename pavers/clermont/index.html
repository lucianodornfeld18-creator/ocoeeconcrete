Paver Contractors Serving Clermont

Clermont's rolling hills and lakefront neighborhoods create some of the most visually rewarding paver projects in our area. Homes in communities like Legends, Lakeview Preserve and Verde Ridge benefit enormously from quality paver driveways and outdoor living spaces. Lake County's sandy soils make thorough base compaction essential — we excavate to the correct depth and use geotextile fabric where needed to prevent base migration, a step critical in Clermont's terrain.

We use a 32-point installation checklist on every project: proper excavation depth, compacted crusher-run base, rigid edge restraints and polymeric joint sand. These steps — which many low-bid contractors skip — are what separates an installation that lasts 30+ years from one that sinks and shifts in 5.
