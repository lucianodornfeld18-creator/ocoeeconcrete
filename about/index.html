Who We Are

Central Florida's Trusted Paver & Concrete Specialists

Ocoee Concrete & Pavers is a locally owned hardscaping contractor based in Ocoee, FL. We specialize in paver driveways, patios, walkways and pool decks, as well as concrete driveways, slabs and epoxy floor coatings.

Our service area covers all of Central Florida — from Ocoee, Winter Garden and Windermere in the west to Orlando, Kissimmee and Davenport in the south, and Apopka and Clermont in the north.

With over 500 driveways and hardscaping projects completed across Central Florida, we've built a reputation for showing up on time, communicating clearly, delivering clean installs, and standing behind our work.

We use quality interlocking concrete pavers with proper compacted crushed-stone bases, polymeric joint sand and rigid edge restraints — the same techniques used on commercial projects — on every residential install. No shortcuts.
